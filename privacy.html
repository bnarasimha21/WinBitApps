Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Our Commitment

We believe your data is yours. Our apps are designed with privacy as a core principle, not an afterthought. We collect only what's absolutely necessary to provide the service, and we never sell your data to third parties.

Data Collection by App

DailyBrick (Habit Tracker)

  • Data stored: Habit names, completion dates, settings
  • Storage: 100% on your device (CoreData)
  • Cloud: Optional iCloud backup (stored in YOUR iCloud, not ours)
  • Analytics: None
  • Account required: No

FlipIt (Memory Game)

  • Data collected: Display name (for leaderboards), game scores
  • Storage: Firebase (for leaderboards only)
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics (anonymous usage stats)
  • Account: Optional Google sign-in for leaderboards

TripXplorer

  • Data collected: Trip details, preferences
  • Storage: Device + optional cloud sync
  • Third parties: OpenAI (for AI features), Mapbox (for maps)
  • Account: Required for cloud features
